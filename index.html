What we do

Hackathons

We as a society regularly participate in Hackathons and other type of competitions. We enjoy doing these types of events as it allows anyone from all sorts of skillsets and backgrounds to create projects, which we all can be proud of.

Social events

Often we all go out to events as a society. Such as going out to: clubs, pubs, restaurants; whatever it be, we all want to have a good time.

Industry talks

The university often hosts weekly industry talks, where we can all learn from people's experience in their industry.

Trips

We like to go and visit places as a society. Whether it be educational or not, we want to make sure everyone has a fun time, and enjoyed their day out.
